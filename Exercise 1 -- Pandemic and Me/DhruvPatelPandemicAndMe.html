The COVID-19 pandemic has certainly changed the lives of millions across the world. Here is how my life has specifically been changed in the past year:

Education. My academic life has been greatly influenced by the pandemic, with my high school shutting down in-person operations and shifting to a remote format. Distance learning through Zoom, it’s been difficult at times to effectively communicate with teachers and other students - nonetheless, I’ve been using office hours to address this issue.

Sports represent the second aspect of my life that has been changed due to the pandemic. Prior to the pandemic, I was actively involved with a local, recreational basketball league, but our season was curtailed due to the current public health circumstances. However, our team has adapted, connecting via Zoom calls and independently working out at home.

Family is the last major aspect that the pandemic has influenced. With the pandemic, much of my family stays at home, remotely learning or working. Engaging with such has allowed us all to be able to spend time with one another and that's been one of the few positive sides of the pandemic!
